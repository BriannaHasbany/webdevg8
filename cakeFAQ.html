Frequently Asked Questions

We take orders either by phone, email, or through our online form. Please give as much lead time as possible. If you have a short-notice order, ask us — we’ll try to make it happen! After submitting your online order, please call us to confirm. Thank you.

Because all of our cakes are priced individually by the complexity of their design, we do not have a standard price list. Each cake is a one-of-a-kind creation, made with care and attention to detail.

To start the order process, please fill out the order form or email us at cakesbydesignedibleart@hotmail.com with:

  • The date of your event
  • The occasion (wedding, birthday, etc.)
  • Event location, delivery address, and delivery time
  • Number of guests
  • Your daytime phone number and email address
  • Any cake inspiration or reference images
  • Two flavors you'd like to sample

We will contact you to discuss your order or schedule an appointment. If you don't hear back, please call us — it can get busy! Every cake we make is custom-made from start to finish.

We can print your pictures, corporate logos, or images onto cakes, cupcakes, and cookies! Displaying your logo on one of our designs is a great way to brand your company. Picture cakes and cupcakes are popular for corporate events, retirements, charity events, and anniversaries.

We can make display cakes perfect for bridal events, outdoor weddings, or magazine shoots. Please note: much of the cost lies in the decoration time and detail — whether the base is real cake or Styrofoam. Most cakes can be replicated, though there may be small variations since each design is unique.

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to reserve your order; the remaining balance is due at least two weeks before your event. Your order is confirmed once we’ve received your signed contract and deposit.

Payment can be made by cash, check, or credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, or Discover). Deposits are non-refundable. Balance payments are refundable only if cancellation is made at least seven (7) business days before the event.

Yes! Delivery is available for all cakes, especially those requiring on-site assembly. Delivery fees are based on distance from North Andover, MA, and we proudly deliver with Paramount Delivery Service.

Longer deliveries may be arranged on a case-by-case basis. Self-transporting smaller cakes is usually fine, but we do not recommend transporting large, 3D, or multi-tier cakes yourself. Cakes by Design Edible Art is not responsible for any damage after pickup.

We do not ship our cakes.
