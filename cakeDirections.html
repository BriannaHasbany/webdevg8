We are located in downtown North Andover, just off the main street for easy access.

Parking is available behind the shop, and our front entrance is wheelchair accessible.

We recommend calling ahead if you need directions or have a large order pickup.

From the South (Route 93 and Boston)

  • Take Route 93 North to Exit 42 (Route 125 North).
  • Turn right at the end of the ramp. Follow Route 125 approximately 7 miles and turn left at the traffic light at the intersection with Route 114
    • Royal Crest Apartments are directly across the way. Merrimack College will be on your left
  • At the next traffic light, turn right, continuing on Route 125. Go straight and turn right at the traffic light after the 99Restaurant (Mass. Ave.).
  • Go through North Andover's Old Center. We are located next to the rotary in the Hay Scales Building on the left hand side.

From the West and Route 495

  • Take Route 495 to Exit 43 (Mass. Ave. - North Andover).
    • Turn right if coming from the South. Turn left if coming from the North.
  • Follow for 2 miles straight through traffic lights to Old North Andover Center.
  • We are located next to the rotary in the Hay Scales Building on the left hand side.
