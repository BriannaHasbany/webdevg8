Welcome to Cakes by Design Edible Art - home of bright, bold and delicious customized cakes. Our Founder
Chef Lina Hunter e was born in Colombia, South America. After graduating from dentistry school in the year 1996, she ran a dental practice for 11 years. Despite working in a different industry, she always wanted to bake as a pastry chef. Whether it was chocolates, cookies or cakes, she loved baking for family and school events.
She then decided to pursue her passion by studying with great confectionary artists like Scott Clark Woolley and Colette Peters at the Notter School of Pastry Arts. Using her fun and colorful approach, she became a master of appearance and taste when it came to baked goods . She then founded Cakes by Design Edible Art in 2005.
As the business grew, she brought together a team of exceptionally talented artists and talent that broadened Cakes by Designs' portfolio and grew into one big happy cakemaking family.
Our inspiration comes from everywhere: art, fashion, fabric, furniture, architecture, landscapes, science, music, and history. Most of all, our inspiration comes from each one of our incredible clients. Every cake we create is custom made from concept to consumption and is a completely original piece of edible art. If you can imagine it, we can create it.
