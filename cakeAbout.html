Our Story

Chef Lina Hunter was born in Colombia, South America. After graduating from dentistry school in 1996, she ran a dental practice for 11 years. Even while working in another field, she always found herself baking — crafting chocolates, cookies, and cakes for family and community events.

Following her passion, Lina trained under world-renowned confectionery artists including Scott Clark Woolley and Colette Peters at the Notter School of Pastry Arts. There, she discovered her true calling: creating edible art that inspires joy through color, flavor, and imagination.

In 2005, she founded Cakes by Design Edible Art — a bakery built on kindness, peace, love, and happiness. What started as one woman’s creative dream has grown into a team of talented artists who blend fine art and flavor to craft one-of-a-kind cakes that celebrate life’s sweetest moments.

Our inspiration comes from everywhere:

  • Art
  • Fashion
  • Fabric
  • Furniture
  • Architecture
  • Landscapes
  • Science
  • Music
  • History

Most of all, our inspiration comes from each one of our incredible clients. Every cake we create is custom made from concept to consumption and is a completely original piece of edible art.

If you can imagine it, we can create it.
